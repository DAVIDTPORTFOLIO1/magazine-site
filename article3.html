An Unplanned Moment

This photo was never planned.

I was just sitting by the lake, minding my own business, when I happened to look up and noticed something that felt a bit out of place. Not in a bad way — just unusual enough to catch my attention.

A seagull was sat on a single plank of wood, floating quietly on the lake.

There was no drama to it. No movement, no noise. It was just there, almost as if it had chosen that exact spot on purpose.

I grabbed the camera and took the shot straight away. One of those times where you don’t think, you just react.

Looking back, that’s probably why I like this image so much. It wasn’t staged or waited for. It was just a moment you’d normally glance at and move on from.

Sometimes the photos you don’t plan are the ones that stick with you the most.
