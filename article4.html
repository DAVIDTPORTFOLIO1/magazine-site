Rods by the Water

At first glance this is just a simple shot of rods by the water.

There was no big idea behind it and nothing particularly planned. I just liked the scene and took the photo.

What makes it interesting to me is the narrow depth of field. When I look at it now, my eyes don’t immediately settle on one rod.

It almost feels slightly confusing, like you’re not quite sure which part of the image you’re meant to focus on.

That wasn’t intentional at the time, but I think that’s why the photo works. It makes you pause for a second instead of just glancing and moving on.
