Quiet moments on the water

Great Crested Grebe

This photo is of a great crested grebe, a bird I’ve always thought was particularly beautiful.

They sit low in the water and when they dive it all feels very smooth and deliberate, almost effortless.

I’m lucky on my syndicate to have a few breeding pairs, so I get to watch them regularly while I’m waiting for a rod to spring into life.

Those quiet moments, just watching what’s happening on the lake, are a big part of why I enjoy being there.
